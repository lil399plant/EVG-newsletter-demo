About the EVG Newsletter Committee Columbia University

The EVG Newsletter Committee is a student-run team at Columbia University dedicated to producing the EVG Weekly Newsletter — a markets-centric publication covering equities, rates, foreign exchange, commodities, and digital assets.

Each issue delivers institutional-quality analysis written by Columbia students, organized across five asset classes with a focus on actionable investment ideas and real-world market commentary.

Committee members develop core finance skills in financial modeling, sector research, and written communication — building the foundation for careers in investment banking, sales & trading, and asset management.

The Enterprise Valuation Group (EVG) is a broader student organization at Columbia University focused on developing rigorous investment analysis and valuation skills among its members. Follow us on Instagram ↗
